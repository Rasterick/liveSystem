What does Sun Tzu say about knowledge?

Deep knowledge is to be aware of disturbance before disturbance, to be aware of danger before danger, to be aware of destruction before destruction, to be aware of calamity before calamity.

Seamless Integration

Integration involves strategically aligning with circumstances rather than forcing a solution, treating the entire situation as a dynamic whole to achieve victory with minimal conflict. This concept, which can be applied to business or warfare, means adapting to the "flow" of a situation and integrating all variables to find the optimal path forward. Key ideas include avoiding direct confrontation when possible, understanding your enemy, and capturing what you can intact rather than destroying it.
